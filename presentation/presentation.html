meetNow

is about simplifying meeting organization


Discover now ...

The challenge:

meet friends or colleagues on a regular basis

The solution:

join the meeting
when all spare time and are just around the corner

To achieve this simplification


we use the ionic 2 framework and various plug-ins, e.g.:

Background Geolocation, OneSignal, Social Sharing, Google Maps

Background Geolocation

function: track position of app user

return notification when geofence is entered

OneSignal

function: broadcast information to app user

gain: easy communication between devices

Google Maps

function: define a meeting location

gain: known usability and trusted design

Social Sharing I

function: share a deeplink to invite new contacts

gain: open the meetNow contact list on the receivers device

Social Sharing II

function: inform participants about the event

send via whatsapp, mail or message

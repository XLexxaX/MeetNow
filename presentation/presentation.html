Meet Now

You need to meet a person regularly?

You wanna meet your best friend bi-weekly?

You want to meet them when all have free time.
And when you and your colleagues are just around the corner?

Here is the solution:

Let "Meet Now" schedule your regular meetings for you!

And never miss to keep your relationships on good terms again.

MeetNow uses plug-ins for:

  • Geofence to locate you and your collegues
  • Social media to add your friends to the app and share meeting information

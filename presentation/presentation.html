meetNow

is about simplifying meeting organization


Discover now ...

The challenge:

meet friends or colleagues on a regular basis

The solution:

join the meeting
when all spare time and are just around the corner

To achieve this simplification


we use the ionic 2 framework
and various plug-ins, e.g.:

Background Geolocation, OneSignal, Social Sharing,
Google Maps, Cordova Calendar

Invite Friends

function: share a deeplink to invite new contacts

gain: open the meetNow contact list on the receivers device and send notification back to sender when added

Plan a meeting

function: define a meeting location via Google Maps and set parameters for duration, frequency and list attendees

Receive a meeting request

Using OneSignal: broadcast information to app user

gain: easy communication between devices

Start a meeting

Using Background Geolocation : track position of app user and send push notification

request for action and starts the meeting

Share a meeting

function: inform participants about the event

send via whatsapp, mail or message

Meeting starts

function: use device calendar to identify free and busy times

gain: block a time slot while the meeting take place
